SAN HONG [홍산; 洪山]

Currently a Ph.D. student at Life Enhancing Technology Lab, Seoul National University!

About

My primary interest lies in accommodations, adaptations, and modifications of environments for minority populations. Exploring human factors of people who haven’t had a chance to own tools and languages to communicate, I wish to bridge those and able-centered standardization. I believe good design comes from understanding users’ life context, resulting in pursuit of inclusion.

Research Interest

Neurodiversity, CNS Disorders, Biomechanics, Inclusive Design, Health Equity
