Statement

My primary interest lies in accommodations, adaptations, and modifications of the environments for minority populations. Exploring human factors of people who haven’t had the chance to own tools and languages to communicate, I wish to bridge those and able-centered standardization. I believe good design comes from understanding users’ life context, resulting in the pursuit of inclusion.
